About the Role

As a Senior Research Scientist at Encode Life, you will be at the forefront of bioplastic innovation. You will lead research and development projects aimed at creating the next generation of sustainable materials. This role is critical to our mission of replacing conventional plastics with eco-friendly alternatives, and you will have a direct impact on shaping a greener future.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and execute experiments for polymer synthesis and characterization.
  • Analyze data, interpret results, and present findings to cross-functional teams.
  • Develop and optimize manufacturing processes for polylactic acid (PLA).
  • Mentor junior scientists and contribute to a culture of scientific excellence.
  • Stay updated with the latest advancements in biotechnology and material science.

Required Qualifications

  • Ph.D. in Chemistry, Material Science, or a related field.
  • 5+ years of hands-on experience in polymer research and development.
  • Proven track record of successful project leadership and innovation.
  • Expertise in analytical techniques such as GPC, DSC, and TGA.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
  • Comprehensive health insurance for you and your family.
  • Opportunities for professional development and continuous learning.
  • Flexible working hours and a hybrid work model.
  • A collaborative and innovative work environment focused on sustainability.

About the Team

Our Research & Development team is a passionate group of 15 scientists and engineers dedicated to pushing the boundaries of material science. We foster a culture of curiosity, collaboration, and impact. We believe in open communication and value diverse perspectives to solve complex challenges together.
